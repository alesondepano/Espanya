About Españya

The heart of student life along Españya Boulevard

Españya Boulevard is the vibrant heart of Manila’s University Belt, a bustling corridor teeming with students, historic universities, and a lively urban energy that never sleeps. Along this iconic stretch, you’ll find a dynamic mix of academic institutions, cozy cafés, colorful street food stalls, affordable eateries, and bookstores catering to generations of students and locals alike.

From iconic campuses like UST, FEU, NU, and UE to affordable eateries and bookstores, Españya connects culture, education, and daily life, making it the ultimate student hub in Manila.
